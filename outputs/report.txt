  INSIGHT 5 – Discount Impact: Orders with 21–30% discount have 0.1% lower profit margins than non-discounted orders. Recommendation: Cap discounts at 10% unless clearing overstock.

  INSIGHT 6 – Customer Concentration: Top 10 customers account for 2.7% of total revenue ($139,278). Recommendation: Build a VIP retention programme for these accounts.

  INSIGHT 7 – YoY Growth: Revenue grew -4.4% from 2023 to 2024. Recommendation: Audit customer churn and pricing strategy.
